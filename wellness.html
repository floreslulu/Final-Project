Wellness and Healthy Living

Wellness is about creating balance through healthy habits that support physical, mental, and emotional well-being.

Healthy Daily Habits

  1. Morning Routine
    • Drink water
    • Stretch or take a short walk
    • Practice deep breathing
  2. Evening Routine
    • Limit screen time
    • Reflect or journal
    • Get 7–8 hours of sleep

Nutrition

Eating nourishing foods supports energy levels, concentration, and long-term health.

Movement

Daily movement such as walking, stretching, or exercise supports physical strength and mental clarity.

Mental and Emotional Health

Mindfulness, rest, and emotional balance are essential parts of overall wellness.
